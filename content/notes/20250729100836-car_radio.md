## RDS (Radio Data System) {#rds--radio-data-system}

It is a standard for transmitting additional information along with FM radio broadcasts.
RDS enhances the functionality of FM radios by providing metadata and improving user experience.
Features of RDS include:

1.  **AF** (Alternative Frequency) for seamless signal switching.
2.  **TA** (Traffic Announcement) for traffic updates.
3.  **PTY** (Program Type) to categorize stations (e.g., news, music, sports).
4.  **PS** (Program Service Name) to display the station name.


## Radio car adapter {#radio-car-adapter}

Using a radio car adapter is a simple way to reproduce personal music on a car that doesn't have an aux-in port.
This plug into the standard lighter port and can connect to a device like a phone through bluetooth or cable.
They emit a radio signal at a selected frequency that the car radio can pick up and reproduce.


### Possible problems {#possible-problems}

Since this simple devices only broadcast data without implementing [RDS (Radio Data System)](#rds--radio-data-system), some of the automatic features could result in unexpected behavior.
**AF** seems to act like it's not receiving a signal, changing to a different bandwith, it's a good idea to disable it.
