## Bloob.io {#bloob-dot-io}

A hub for quick multiplayer word, strategy, and board-style games—try Wordrace, Battleships, Connect Four etc.


## Gartic Phone {#gartic-phone}

Link: <https://garticphone.com/>
 A drawing-based game of “telephone” (pass the message), where you alternate between drawing and describing.


## OpenGuessr {#openguessr}

Link: <https://openguessr.com/>
Free and open alternative to geoguesser.
A game where you have to guess where are you located based on google streetview images.
