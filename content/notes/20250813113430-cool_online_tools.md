## Timeful {#timeful}

link: <https://timeful.app/>
Coordinate group meetings with a shared calendar to match availability.


## I Love PDF {#i-love-pdf}

link: <https://www.ilovepdf.com/>
Pretty much all the tools that you could need to handle pdf files
Merge, split, edit, transform, sign etc.


## Jitsi {#jitsi}

link: <https://meet.jit.si/>
Open source video conference and meeting software


## One time secret {#one-time-secret}

link: <https://onetimesecret.com/>
Encrypt messages for easy sharing of compromising data with a one time use link


## Cyber Cheff {#cyber-cheff}

link: <https://gchq.github.io/CyberChef/>
Great compilation of online tools for encrypting, decrypting, and transforming different formats, as well as forensic tools.
Usefull for CTF competitions and other cybersecurity activities.


## OpenCut (beta) {#opencut--beta}

link: <https://opencut.app/>
Opensource online video editor


## Graphite {#graphite}

link: <https://graphite.rs/>
Opensource vector and raster graphics editor


## Photopea {#photopea}

link: <https://www.photopea.com/>
Online photo editing software, with support for a variety of formats including .pds


## osint.sh Certificate Search {#osint-dot-sh-certificate-search}

Search certificates for a domain, it's an easy way to find subdomains


## tilealchemy {#tilealchemy}

link: <https://tilealchemy.com/>
Generate and design tiled patterns


## fffuel {#fffuel}
